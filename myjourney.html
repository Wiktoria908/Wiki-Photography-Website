How It Began

I always enjoyed taking photos however I have never been so passionate about my images and I have never fully considered to make it a hobby and go deeper into photography. It all began in 2019 when I started a course called Creative Digital Media in TuDublin University. I have always used my phone on automatic photo mod but it all changed when I held a professional camera in my hands.

Moving On

I got very inetrested in photography since day one however at first I felt a bit overwhelmed by all the different settings and never thought Photography will become my passion. The first photography assignment that we had was a big starting point for me where I started to look deeper and experiment. I moved on from taking just a nice photo but instead I started to search looking for inspiration to make my photos meaningful.

"Point and Shoot"

Photography is not something you can just learn. Knowing how to use the camera is not everything as you there's plenty of new skills to learn and you can never stop exploring more. I think this is one of the reason why I enjoy photography so much because there's always so much more you can do and never get bored. You can look beyond the horizon and capture the unseen, then point and shoot.
